Release is heading to Las Vegas for [**Google Cloud Next '24**](https://cloud.withgoogle.com/next) (April 9-11). We’re looking forward to sharing product updates, meeting with the community and seeing how Google will shape the future of cloud technology. From artificial intelligence applications to infrastructure solutions, Google Cloud Next '24 represents a new chapter in digital transformation.

Stop by the Release booth (#1202), where we'll be showcasing our latest products and advancements, including our [early access program for Release.ai](http://release.ai).

## **What to Expect at Google Cloud Next '24**

With over 400 sessions, Google Cloud Next, attendees can expect valuable insights, interactive sessions, and networking opportunities. We’re looking forward to sessions focused on:
*   AI and ML
*   Application Developers
*   Infrastructure Architecture and IT Operators
*   Data Analysts, Data Scientists, Data Engineers
*   Database Professionals
*   DevOps, ITOps, Platform Engineers, SREs
*   IT Managers and Business Leaders
*   Productivity and Collaboration
*   Security Professionals

## **Who Should Attend Google Cloud Next ‘24**

Google Cloud Next is tailored to cater to a diverse range of individuals, including:
*   IT professionals and managers seeking to enhance their knowledge and skills.
*   Software developers and architects aiming to stay updated with the latest advancements.
*   Cloud engineers and consultants dedicated to optimizing cloud infrastructure.
*   Business leaders who aspire to harness the power of cloud technologies for both growth and innovation.
